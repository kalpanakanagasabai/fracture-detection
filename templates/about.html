About Us

Our Wrist Fracture Detection System is designed to assist healthcare professionals by automating the detection and classification of wrist fractures using advanced deep learning technology. The system analyzes X-ray images to: Detect fractures with high accuracy. Classify fractures into types such as complete, incomplete, and dislocations. Identify specific fracture names, like scaphoid or Colles fractures. This innovative tool aims to improve diagnostic efficiency, reduce human error, and support better treatment planning.
