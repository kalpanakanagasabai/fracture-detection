Understanding Wrist Fracture Types

Colles' Fracture

Dorsal displacement of the distal radius, often from a fall on an outstretched hand.

Smith's Fracture

Volar displacement of the distal radius, typically from a fall on a flexed wrist.

Barton's Fracture

Intra-articular fracture of the distal radius with radiocarpal joint dislocation.

Chauffeur's Fracture

Fracture of the radial styloid process, commonly from forceful impact.

Scaphoid Fracture

Fracture of the scaphoid bone, often difficult to diagnose, treat promptly.

BeFunky Fracture

Lunate bone fracture, can lead to serious complications if missed.
